# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in Fiducial Mesh a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Critique the work, not the person. Adversarial review of *artifacts* is the method here; adversarial treatment of *people* is not.
- Ground disagreement in evidence — the spec text, the code, the data.
- Give and accept correction gracefully; a finding against your work is the process succeeding, not a personal failure.
- Show empathy and respect toward other contributors.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal or political attacks, publishing others' private information, and other conduct reasonably considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or unacceptable behavior may be reported privately to the project maintainers. Maintainers will review and respond, and are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter. Maintainers who do not follow or enforce this Code of Conduct in good faith may face repercussions as determined by the project's leadership.
